About the Symposium

Uniting Cambodia & Switzerland in advancing pediatric and medical excellence

🌏 International Collaboration

The 10th International Kantha Bopha Symposium unites leading medical specialists from Cambodia and Switzerland to share clinical advances, surgical innovations, and best practices in pediatric care.

🏥 About Kantha Bopha

Kantha Bopha Children's Hospital and Jayavarman VII Hospital are Cambodia's leading pediatric hospitals, providing free high-quality medical care to hundreds of thousands of children every year.

🔬 Medical Excellence

Showcasing groundbreaking clinical work in surgery, oncology, cardiology, neonatology, nephrology and more — reflecting extraordinary medical achievements of Kantha Bopha hospitals.

📍 Siem Reap – Angkor

Held at Jayavarman VII Hospital near the world-famous Angkor Wat temples — a landmark of Khmer civilization and Cambodian national pride.
